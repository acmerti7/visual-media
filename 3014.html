COMM 3014- Public Relations Cases

Dr. Logan

In PR Cases, we analyze different case studies so that we may learn from them. This is an incredibly important class because it involves researching and understanding the principles of public relations practice.

Dr. Logan is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Communication. She earned her B.A. from the University of Georgia and her M.A. and Ph.D. from Georgia State University. She has received three awards, including the 2016 Top Faculty Paper Award, Public Relations Division from the National Communication Association. Also, she received the Golden Flame Award in Communication Management/Strategic Communication Processes from the International Association of Business Communicators in 2012 and in 2006 was awarded the Phoenix Award Certificate for Excellence in the Magazine Category for XChange Magazine: Hurricane Katrina Special Issue by the Public Relations Society of America Atlanta Chapter.
