COMM 2034- Visual Media

Dr. Evia

Visual Media involves learning how to use different Adobe software and HTML to create various media. Our projects have required us to create videos, websites, and publications. I have particularly enjoyed this class because most of the projects are collaborative, so I got to work on them with my friends.

Dr. Evia is my Visual Media professor. He earned his L.C.C. from the Instituto de Ciencias Sociales de Merida, his M.S.C. from the Universidad LaSalle, and his Ph.D. from Texas Tech University. Dr. Evia received the Frank R. Smith Award for Distinguished Article in the Journal Technical Communication in 2017 and has been recognized for Outstanding Faculty Outreach by Virginia Tech's College of Liberal Arts and Human Sciences in 2011.
